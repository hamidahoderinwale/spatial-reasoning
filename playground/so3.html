SO(3) playground

The pictorial counterpart to the SO(2) tool. Drag the scene to orbit; rotate B with the sliders; toggle mirror. A proper rotation (det = +1) keeps B congruent to A (“same”); a reflection (det = −1) is the enantiomer no rotation can superimpose (“mirror”). The det sign is the chirality bit.
